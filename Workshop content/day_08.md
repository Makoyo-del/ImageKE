## Morning Post (Educational)

### LinkedIn Post
Applying for jobs without an AI workflow is like writing a book on a typewriter. It is slow, inefficient, and puts you at a disadvantage.

Many job seekers spend hours manually matching keywords and proofreading every application. Meanwhile, their peers are using modern tools to double their efficiency and target roles accurately.

AI is not replacing job seekers; it is separating those who use it strategically from those who do not. The goal is to automate the repetitive tasks so you can focus on interview prep and networking.

A modern AI workflow has four simple steps: (1) Extract core requirements from the job description, (2) Compare your master CV to find gaps, (3) Draft direct, metric-driven achievements, and (4) Format for ATS compliance.

I've reviewed dozens of CVs, and the candidates who use a structured AI workflow submit higher-quality applications in a fraction of the time.

Stop doing manual labor that a machine can assist with. Learn the correct system.
